About Dela

Having a BA in Politics and International Relations, I have always had a passion to understand current affairs. However, whilst studying I was also a professional makeup artist. I saw every face as a blank canvas, experimenting with different colours, textures, and styles. Constantly broadening my horizons.I have now entered a chapter in my life in which I want to create timeless art. Paintings that will never fade in meaning and always spark a feeling.
