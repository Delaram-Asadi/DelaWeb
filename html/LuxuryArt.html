Luxury Art

Cutomised fine art pieces are the ones that take both the longest to create but also the most intricate to create.

Each piece is a limited creation & fusion of chemical reactions and painting. Painting is a multi layer process, which gives these pieces a multidimensional and polished look. Due to this, they tend to reflect the warmth and colours of the room they are in. The longer you look, the more you will find in the details. Pushing the boundaries of minimalism but a few colours and creating a intricate piece.
