Acrylic Pouring is a fascinating fluid painting technique. Mainly using acrylic paint, which can be brought into a liquid with pourable consistency. T he technique is to liquefie the paint and then pour into each other onto the canvas and distributed by tilting the painting surface. The process creates an adbrast effect however with use of high heat and fire, a fine chemical reaction takes place making the colours more vibrant and adding a special effect.
